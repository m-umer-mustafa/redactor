Support

Billing & Subscriptions

Everything you need to know about your Redactor account.

How does recurring billing work?

Your subscription is billed monthly via **Whop**, our secure payment partner. You will be charged on the same day every month. If you started with a 7-day free trial, your first billing cycle begins immediately after the trial ends.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime with zero hassle. Simply click the Manage Subscription link in our header or visit Whop.com/hub. Once signed in, you can terminate your plan in two clicks. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Where is my license key?

After purchase, you will receive an automated email from Whop with your unique key. You can also find all your active keys in your Whop Dashboard under the "Software" tab.

Can I change my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade from Solo to Pro at any time through the Management Portal. Your billing will be pro-rated automatically.
